# Xavier's Hybrid Developer Curriculum

30-week self-study curriculum to transition from Power Platform developer to Hybrid Developer in the Belgian enterprise market.

## Background

About 4 years ago I learned JavaScript/Node/Express, C#/.NET, and worked with PostgreSQL and other databases. Life happened, coding took a back seat, and the skills went rusty.

Fast forward to today — working as a Power Platform developer at NMBS/SNCB, I decided to pick it back up properly. Rather than just refreshing what I knew, the goal became building a hybrid skillset: traditional backend development combined with Power Platform expertise. Most developers don't touch Power Platform. Most Power Platform specialists can't write production APIs. The combination is rare in the Belgian enterprise market — and that's the angle.

Python was assessed and dropped early — JavaScript/TypeScript was the stronger foundation and the more relevant path. Everything else followed from there.
